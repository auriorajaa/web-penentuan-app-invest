Frequently Asked Questions

Our website is designed to help beginner investors select the best investment applications based on comprehensive research and analysis. This is in line with our academic paper, "Sistem Pengambilan Keputusan untuk Memilih Aplikasi Investasi Terbaik bagi Pengguna Pemula," which aims to provide essential knowledge and tools for making informed investment decisions.

Our decision-making system is based on the Simple Additive Weighting (SAW) technique. This method involves evaluating multiple criteria such as ease of use, educational features, transaction costs, security, customer support, and availability of investment instruments to rank the best investment applications for beginner investors.

Data is collected through surveys and questionnaires that target users to evaluate various criteria of investment apps. The responses are then analyzed using the Simple Additive Weighting (SAW) technique to rank the investment apps and provide recommendations based on the aggregated data.
